Biography

There are many things I love in life. Cats. Writing. Chocolate. Coding.

Wait, what?! That's right. I love coding. That phrase still shocks me, just four weeks after this incredible discovery. I stumbled upon my love of coding recently when feeling that my career options were forcing me in a direction that I did not want to go. I thought I might learn wordpress, a thought that led me to download an app on my phone that teaches html. I mastered the app in about a day. Hungry for more, I found and completed the html&css course on codecademy.

Every day I was more surprised at how much I LOVED doing the codecademy courses, a love that was intensified as I made projects on my own. I went on to dabble in the JavaScript, jQuery, and Git courses on codecademy. While these courses were more challenging, I found that I enjoyed them just as much. It was around this point in my journey that I decided that a bootcamp really would be a great experience for me.
